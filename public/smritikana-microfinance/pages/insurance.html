MFI Insurance Compliance

Navigating the RBI 2025 mandates on borrower protection and credit life insurance.

The 2025 Insurance Mandate

Under the new RBI Directions, insurance is a critical pillar of borrower protection. While MFIs can facilitate insurance, the focus has shifted to transparency and the prohibition of predatory practices.

Key Regulatory Clause

"The MFI shall ensure that the insurance coverage is limited to the outstanding loan amount and the period of coverage is co-terminus with the tenure of the loan." - RBI Master Direction, 2025.

Credit Life Insurance

Mandatory for all microfinance loans. We help you partner with IRDAI-regulated insurers to provide seamless coverage for the primary borrower and spouse.

Administrative Charge Ban

As per 2025 rules, MFIs cannot charge any administrative fee for facilitating insurance. Only the actual premium is to be collected.
